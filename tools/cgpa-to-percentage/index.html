Understand SPPU Conversions

Savitribai Phule Pune University (SPPU) uses specific, range-based formulas to convert your CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average) to an equivalent percentage, as outlined in their Official Circular No. 332-2020. This tool helps you quickly perform these calculations to accurately determine your percentage, awarded grade, and class based on your CGPA.
