FAQs – Frequently Asked Questions

What is SPPU Engineers?

SPPU Engineers is an academic platform that helps SPPU students access organized notes, previous year question papers, decode PDFs, and essential study resources.

What resources are available for free?

We provide sample notes, previous year papers, and cheat sheets for free. Full access is available through our affordable premium plans.

Are the materials specific to SPPU?

Yes, all our materials are curated as per the latest SPPU syllabus and pattern to ensure maximum academic benefit.

Will I get notes for all subjects?

Absolutely. We cover all semester subjects for engineering and diploma students under SPPU, regularly updating the library.

What formats are the notes provided in?

Notes are available as downloadable PDFs – including decoded textbook content, short notes, and question banks.

How do I access the study materials?

After joining a plan, you'll receive access via your registered email or WhatsApp. Manual login credentials will also be shared if applicable.
