About SPPU Engineers

SPPU Engineers is an independent student-led initiative built to simplify academics and boost student careers for those under Savitribai Phule Pune University. We're not officially affiliated with SPPU, but we're here to support students where it truly matters.

Our Purpose

We noticed a gap in academic support, career guidance, and real-world opportunities for SPPU students. Our mission is to bridge that gap with curated resources, smart tools, and a community-driven approach. From verified notes to career prep tools — we aim to make your journey easier and smarter.
