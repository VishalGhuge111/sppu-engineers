Our Copyright Policy

At SPPU Engineers, we deeply respect intellectual property rights. If you believe your copyrighted material has been used on our platform without proper authorization, we encourage you to submit a formal claim by following the steps below:

  1. Identify the copyrighted work: Please provide a clear and detailed description of the copyrighted work you claim has been infringed. This helps us accurately locate the content.
  2. Provide the URL(s): Include the precise URL(s) where the allegedly infringing content appears on the SPPU Engineers platform.
  3. Include your contact information: Furnish your full legal name, email address, physical address, and a reliable telephone number. This information is crucial for us to communicate with you regarding your claim.
  4. Statement of good faith: You must include a statement affirming that you have a good faith belief that the use of the copyrighted material is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  5. Accuracy statement: Include a statement, made under penalty of perjury, that the information in your notification is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the owner of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed.

Upon receipt of a complete and valid copyright infringement notification, we will promptly review the claim and take appropriate actions, which may include removing or disabling access to the infringing material.
